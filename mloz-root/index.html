Picture this: Record your Magical Land of Oz

The ABC natural history series Magical Land of Oz and Atlas of Living Australia (ALA) invite you to become a citizen scientist by recording and submitting sightings of Australian native wildlife that are all around you.

Make a valuable contribution to biodiversity science and collect as many sightings as you can!

It's simple: spot some wildlife and take a photo with your phone, or load a camera photo onto your computer. Then:

  • If you already have an ALA account, go ahead and Upload your sightings.
  • If you are happy to use your Facebook, Google or Twitter account to log in to the ALA, go ahead and Upload your sightings.
  • Otherwise, create an ALA account - it's quick and free - then Upload your sightings.

Your wildlife sightings will help scientists build a more complete picture of Australia's biodiversity. The information will be used by researchers, the government and natural resource managers.
