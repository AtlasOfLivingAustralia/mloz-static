Picture this: Record your Magical Land of Oz

The ABC natural history series Magical Land of Oz and Atlas of Living Australia (ALA) invite you to become a citizen scientist by recording and submitting sightings of Australian native wildlife that are all around you.

Make a valuable contribution to biodiversity science and collect as many sightings as you can!

It's simple:

  1. Spot some wildlife and take a photo with your phone, or load a camera photo onto your computer.
  2. You will need to log in to ALA. You can do this safely with your Facebook, Twitter or Google account, or you can create an ALA account - it's quick and free;
  3. Once you are ready to log in, you can

Your sightings will help scientists to monitor the distribution and movements of species through space and time. The information will be used by policy makers, local government authorities and natural resource managers to improve the management outcomes for species, achieve more effective management of weed and pest incursions, improve urban and infrastructure design for both people and other species, and much more.
